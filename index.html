ABOUT US

Trident SoftLab is the most forward-thinking company in Ukraine that can customize the software production and improve the skills of employees according to the best contemporary requirements of the worlds’ market. Trident SoftLab has 5 years of experience in setting up successful remote and parallel software development teams. We have a team of consultants that focuses on making the work between software development teams in Ukraine and our customers’ organizations better. We have vast experience in processes analysis and improvements within software development companies. Our team always aims to deliver the best services by implementing the high quality solutions which are precisely adapted to the needs of our clients. We offer cost-effective Software Development Teams establishment, support and management services that allow our customers to concentrate on their core business processes and be one step ahead of their competitors. We use the following practices: Classical management, PMI, Scrum, Kanban, Agile, Lean, RUP, Prince2, SAFe, LESS, Nexus and other best practices as well as Agile Scaling. The combined experience of our consultants is over 50 years in Software consulting in largest international companies. Unlike in the traditional project outsourcing model, we help clients to establish their own software development centers in one of our offshore software development locations in Ukraine where they outsource, completely or partially, their software development processes. This is the equivalent of having your in-house IT specialists, but for a significantly lower cost and with less administrative nuisances. Trident SoftLab clients can gain control over their IT processes and systems in order to get more value from the resources they already have. We surround each clients' team with knowledge sharing activities to foster a productive work environment during their entire outsourcing experience, including social events, building strong communication infrastructure, providing financial and administrative support, Q&A and Agile, Business and DevOps consulting services.
